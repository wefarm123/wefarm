Our Features

Innovative solutions for agricultural transparency

Traceability: From Farm to Plate

Know where your food comes from with our blockchain-powered traceability system. By scanning a QR code on your product, you can access:

  • Farm location and cultivation methods
  • Real-time updates on harvesting, storage, and transportation
  • Quality certifications and temperature tracking

No more doubts — just transparent information at your fingertips.

Make timely data-driven agronomic decisions

At WEFARM, we provide actionable insights and recommendations for effective irrigation, disease management, nutrition optimization, and effluent management. Our blockchain-powered platform offers data-driven guidance that helps farmers reduce input usage while maximizing crop yields. With our intuitive system, farmers can achieve exceptional results without requiring extensive agronomic expertise. Every decision is backed by real-time data, ensuring sustainable and profitable farming. smart contract system, transactions are executed automatically based on pre-agreed conditions.

Real-Time Market Insights

Stay ahead with AI-powered market intelligence that analyzes:

  • Real-time crop prices
  • Demand and supply trends
  • Weather forecasts and their impact on crops

Farmers can decide the best time to sell, while buyers can make strategic purchasing decisions. With actionable insights, you minimize risks and maximize profits.
